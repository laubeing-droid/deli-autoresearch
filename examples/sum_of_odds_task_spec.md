# goal
Prove that the sum of the first n odd numbers equals n^2.

# milestones
- Propose candidate proof routes
- Validate at least one route or intermediate claim

# success_criteria

# constraints
- Strict JSON-only agent responses

# conjecture
For every positive integer n, 1 + 3 + 5 + ... + (2n - 1) = n^2.

# known_lemmas
- The nth odd number is 2n - 1
- Mathematical induction is allowed
