# SPEC-230 Requirements: Argument Compiler

## FREQ-230-001
Argument SHALL contain distinct `ArgumentId`.

## FREQ-230-002
Argument SHALL contain claim, basis rule, support facts, source refs, and derivation witness.

## FREQ-230-003
Multiple Arguments MAY support the same Claim.

## FREQ-230-004
Compiler SHALL NOT overwrite multiple derivations by conclusion.

## FREQ-230-005
Compiler SHALL be sound.

## FREQ-230-006
Compiler SHALL be complete.
