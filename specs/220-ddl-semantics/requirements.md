# SPEC-220 Requirements: DDL Semantics

## FREQ-220-001: Norm Activation
Model SHALL define norm activation.

## FREQ-220-002: Norm Activation Components
Norm activation SHALL include condition, time, jurisdiction, and enabled status.

## FREQ-220-003: Obligation Satisfaction and Violation
Model SHALL define obligation satisfaction and violation.

## FREQ-220-004: Prohibition Satisfaction and Violation
Model SHALL define prohibition satisfaction and violation.

## FREQ-220-005: Permission as Positive State
Permission SHALL be a positive normative state.

## FREQ-220-006: Permission NOT Defined as Obligation Absent
Permission SHALL NOT be defined as obligation absence.

## FREQ-220-007: Constitutive Norms No Direct Violation
Constitutive norm SHALL NOT directly produce violation.

## FREQ-220-008: Defense Kind Distinction
Model SHALL distinguish defeater, justification, excuse.

## FREQ-220-009: Burden of Proof Participation
Model SHALL let burden of proof participate in evaluation.

## FREQ-220-010: Four Reparation Modes
Model SHALL define four reparation modes.
