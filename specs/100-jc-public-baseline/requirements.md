# SPEC-100 Requirements

## REQ-100-001
Spec SHALL record three repository complete HEAD SHA.

## REQ-100-002
Spec SHALL inventory formal and JC repositories every tracked file. Control repository (deli-autoresearch) only inventories spec files and reports, not business code.

## REQ-100-003
Spec SHALL record root license, file headers, NOTICE and third-party markers.

## REQ-100-004
Spec SHALL distinguish "currently public" from "future asset policy".

## REQ-100-005
Each tracked path SHALL receive exactly one future classification.

## REQ-100-006
Spec SHALL NOT declare currently public content as trade secret.

## REQ-100-007
Spec SHALL NOT modify licenses.

## REQ-100-008
Spec SHALL NOT move large asset directories.

## REQ-100-009
Low confidence classifications SHALL enter human review.

## REQ-100-010
PATENT_REVIEW public reports SHALL NOT expose new technical details.
